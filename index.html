About us

SaafirTech is an innovative B2B tech company specialized in creating innovative solutions in the travel and tourism industry. With almost 12 years of knowledge and experince in the operations of travel and tourism industry in the MENA region, SaafirTech deeply understand the specific needs of different stakeholders and multiple customer segments to ensure a five-star satisfaction level.

Through our technologies, our clients served over 950,000 happy customers and were able to lead and compete in their markets. Our experince involve building booking systems, inventory management systems, digital interactive e-brochures, full scale OTA website, Travel management system, API/XML integrations, app development, Digital Marketing, Content marketing, review management systems etc.
